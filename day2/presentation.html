In Week 1 you collected and analyzed data yourself. This week, a pipeline collects data on its own, and an AI model makes judgements for you.

Ngayong linggo: awtomatiko ang koleksyon ng data, at may modelong AI na tutulong sa pagsusuri.

Concept 1

What is a pipeline?

A chain of automated steps that moves data from a source to a destination, transforming it along the way — no manual clicks.

Fetch prices→ Score them→ Google Sheet→ Alert

Tool: n8n connects the steps visually. Once built, data keeps arriving on its own.

Concept 2

What is a model?

A program that learns patterns from examples instead of being given exact rules. Show it many labeled pictures; it learns to label a new one — with a confidence score.

Natututo ang modelo mula sa mga halimbawa. Bawat hula ay may confidence — gaano ito kasigurado.

No-code AI

Teachable Machine

  • Create classes (e.g. signed vs unsigned) and add example images.
  • Click Train — no code.
  • Export the model as a .zip and run it in Colab over a whole folder.

Low-confidence guesses (< 80%) go to a human to review. AI assists judgement — it doesn't replace it.
