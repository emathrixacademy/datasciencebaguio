Course Orientation

Welcome! This 3-week course teaches you to collect, clean, analyze, and apply AI to real data. The most important rule: the data is real and it is YOURS — you go out and gather it. Basahin muna ito bago simulan ang Week 1.

Orientation (you are here)→ Week 1→ Week 2→ Week 3

How each week works

Every week is the same shape: ① a short Reading first (concepts, no code), then ② the activities in order — and each activity unlocks the next only after you submit your work (your notebook link, your raw data, and your fieldwork proof). Readings are always open.

Bawat linggo: reading muna, tapos activities na naka-sunod-sunod. Kailangan mo munang i-submit ang isang activity bago mabuksan ang susunod.

The three weeks

Week 1 — Foundations

What is data science, the data workflow, EDA, and why cleaning matters.

  • Activity 1 — Data Science (Form → Colab): answer the intake form, then run a real survey of 15+ people and chart it.
  • Activity 2 — Observe & Count: go out, count real things, build a 30+ row dataset by hand, clean it.
  • Activity 3 — EDA: a full exploratory analysis on the data you collected.

Week 2 — Automation & AI

Pipelines and machine-learning basics.

  • Activity 1 — Stock Analysis: track a real number by hand for 5+ days and compare it to the automated pipeline.
  • Activity 2 — Signature Detection: build a real image dataset (40 signed + 40 unsigned) and train an AI model.

Week 3 — Capstone

Everything end-to-end.

  • Capstone — Form to AI: collect from 15+ real respondents who upload real documents; analyze, classify with your model, and defend it live.

🚶 The fieldwork you must collect

This is a labor-intensive, anti-copy course. In every activity you must go out and gather real data, and prove it:

  • Photo AND short video proof of your fieldwork are both required. No proof = not accepted.
  • Pairs or small groups may collect together, but each student submits their own notebook / writeup.
  • Reduced / desk-based data is allowed only with the teacher's prior permission — message first.
  • The raw collected data (the sheet, the log, the image folder) is a required deliverable, not just the charts.

Kailangan ng litrato AT maikling video bilang patunay ng fieldwork. Walang proof, hindi tanggap.

What you need

  • A Google account (for Google Colab, Forms, Sheets, Drive).
  • A phone to take fieldwork photos + videos.
  • Willingness to go out and talk to real people — that's the course.

Privacy

Only collect data people agree to give. For photos/videos of people, ask their okay first. Don't collect sensitive personal info you don't need. (Same as the Data Privacy notice you accepted at sign-in.)
