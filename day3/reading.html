Capstone: From Data Collection to AI, end to end

Read this first. It ties everything together and explains how your group project is graded. Basahin muna bago simulan ang capstone.

1 · Reading (you are here)→ 2 · Capstone — Form to AI

1. The whole flow in one picture

Google Form + image upload→ Google Sheet→ Colab: analyze + visualize→ Teachable Machine: identify + confidence

Your capstone connects every skill from Weeks 1 & 2 into one product: you collect data with a form (Week 1), store it automatically in a Sheet (Week 2 pipeline idea), analyze and visualize it (Week 1 EDA), and apply an AI model to identify the uploaded images (Week 2 AI).

Tagalog: pinagsasama ng capstone ang lahat ng natutunan — kolekta, imbak, suri, at AI — sa iisang proyekto.

2. Each stage, conceptually

  1. Collect — a short Google Form (application/enrollment) with one image-document upload.
  2. Store — responses + each image's Drive link land in a linked Google Sheet automatically.
  3. Analyze — the notebook reads the Sheet, describes the data, and charts the responses.
  4. Interpret — you write 2–3 sentences on what the charts say.
  5. AI — your Teachable Machine model identifies each uploaded image and reports its confidence; low-confidence ones are flagged for human review.
Ownership note: a form file-upload saves to the form owner's Drive, so the notebook must be run by that same Google account — otherwise it can't read the images.

3. How the rubric works (20 points)

Your grade rewards a complete, honest, end-to-end product, not fancy tricks. The full point breakdown is in the Capstone Guide, but conceptually it covers:

AreaWhat graders look for
Data collectionA working form with an image upload + a linked Sheet of real responses.
Analysis & visualsThe notebook connects to the Sheet and produces clear charts.
InterpretationA written, honest reading of what the data shows.
AI + confidenceEach image identified, with a confidence read-out and review of low-confidence ones.
PresentationA 3-minute story: collected → shown → how well the AI performed.

Tagalog: ang puntos ay para sa kumpleto at tapat na proyekto mula simula hanggang dulo — hindi sa pagiging komplikado.
