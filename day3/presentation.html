Your group product

The 5 stages

  • Collect — a Google Form with one image-document upload.
  • Store — responses + image Drive links land in a Sheet.
  • Analyze — the notebook charts the responses.
  • Interpret — write what the charts say.
  • AI — your model identifies each image + reports confidence.
Important

Ownership of the uploads

A Form file-upload saves to the form owner's Google Drive. Run the notebook with that same account, or it can't read the images.

Magkaiba ang account? Hindi mababasa ng notebook ang mga larawan. Gamitin ang account na may-ari ng form.

How you're graded (20 pts)

Complete & honest, end to end

  • Data collection — working form + linked Sheet of real responses.
  • Analysis & visuals — clear charts from the Sheet.
  • Interpretation — an honest reading of the data.
  • AI + confidence — each image identified, low-confidence flagged.
  • Presentation — a 3-minute story.
