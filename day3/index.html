Day 3 — From Data to Decision

A 3-hour group capstone that combines a slice of every activity. Each group of 3–4 splits into roles that work in parallel, all feeding one shared Google Sheet, then combine and present.

The 4 roles (work in parallel → combine)

1 · Collector

a slice of Day 1 Activity 1 / Day 2 Activity 3

  • Get a few responses fast (group fills it).

3-hour timetable

0:00–0:15Brief & assign — form groups, pick a theme, hand out roles + the assigned practice portal.
0:15–1:15Parallel build (60) — each role works on their slice at the same time.
1:15–2:00Integrate (45) — Analyst points the notebook at the shared Sheet; run clean → correlation → dashboard → 3 questions; Builder connects AppSheet / alert.
2:00–2:40Polish (40) — write 3 findings + 1 recommendation; screenshot the dashboard / app / classifier.
2:40–3:00Dry-run (20) — rehearse the 3-minute story.
Hour 4Presentations & closing — 3 minutes per group.
Hard checkpoint at 1:15. If a role is stuck, hand off the partial output to the group and move on — done-small beats perfect.

What "done" looks like (deliverables)

  • A Google Sheet with collected or scraped data
  • One cleaned dataset + one correlation
  • One EDA dashboard + answers to 3 questions
  • One AI-or-app artifact (a classification result, an AppSheet screen, or an n8n alert)
  • A 3-minute story: what data → what we found → what we'd decide
